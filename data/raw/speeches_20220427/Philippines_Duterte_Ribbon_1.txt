Presidential Communications Operations Office Presidential News Desk SPEECH OF PRESIDENT RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE DURING THE GRAND OPENING OF ACACIA HOTEL DAVAO [Delivered at the Grand Ballroom, Acacia Hotel Davao, Davao City | 22 November 2019] Kindly sit down.
